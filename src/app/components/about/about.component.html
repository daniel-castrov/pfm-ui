DoD Directive 5160-05E, Roles and Responsibilities Associated with the Chemical and Biological Defense Program (CBDP) establishes management and oversight of the CBDP by the USD(AT&L), ATSD(NCB), USD(P), USD(I), Secretaries of The Military Departments, Secretary of The Army, Chairman of The Joint Chiefs of Staff, Defense Threat Reduction Agency, and Joint CBRND PAIO.

The Joint Service Chemical Biological Information System is a web-based platform/database containing cost, schedule, and performance data for the CBRNE programs to assist offices in their oversight and management roles.

Examples of data and information support include:
  • Determination of overall health of CBRNE against objectives.
  • Development of budget submissions and change proposals.
  • DTRA Funds Management.
  • Development of the Program Objective Memorandum.
  • Integration of Program, Planning, Budgeting and Execution functions.
